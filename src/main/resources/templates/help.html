Help & Support

CarFix Rwanda helps customers find verified mechanics, submit requests, and track status. This page explains the main workflow for each account type.

Customer Workflow

1) Register as Customer. 2) Add at least one vehicle. 3) Submit a service request and optionally choose a mechanic. 4) Track or cancel open requests from your dashboard.

Mechanic Workflow

Register as Mechanic with specialization, supported models, and location. After admin approval, your garage appears in the public directory and requests can be assigned to you.

Admin Responsibilities

Admins verify mechanic profiles, assign mechanics for unassigned requests, and update service statuses when needed.
